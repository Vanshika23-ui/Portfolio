A Trip To Moscow and St. Petersburg

I took a trip to russia during the month of june and trust me russia is so beautiful..But there are lot of preparations you need to do before going to russia like planning flight tickets to bus tickets to train tickets to sim cards to luggage and obviously to visa. So i travelled to moscow and St.petersburg where the vast majority of tourists who travel to Russia visit there.It is such a peaceful and marvelous place.Also it account for around 90% of tourist traffic. This percentage also includes all those who visit the Golden Ring of Russia northeast of Moscow. Also there are other tourist destinations are Kamchatka or Altai, Lake Baikal but are a long distance away. These are attractive destinations but for more adventurous travellers.Also russia is such a big place that around half of india can be shifted there.

SOME TIPS ON YOUR TRIP :

Best Time of the Day: Mornings and Evenings

To Carry: A good camera, monopod or tripod, sweaters, drinking water

Best Season: June to December

Mode of Travel: Train, Bike, Bus
